Group Stage

The group stage. Each player plays every player in their group once. Three tournament points are awarded for a win, and zero tournament points for a loss. The three players with the highest tournament points from each group will progress into the knockout phase of the tournament, togther with two of the highest scoring players that are left.

** Total points scored in each match are also added together, in the event of a tie-breaker.
